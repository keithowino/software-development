Descendant Selector

Example:-

This are my descendants: Jackob, Lea, Nebuchadneza and Mr. Ndegwa. They are too many, i almost forgot the others. We have Mercy, Ruth and Kimberly.

The above underlined texts are recognised as the descendants of the p.descendant-selector-eg element.

Child Selector

Example:-

This are my descendants: Jackob, Lea, Nebuchadneza and Mr. Ndegwa. They are too many, i almost forgot the others. We have Mercy, Ruth and Kimberly.

Here only the above underlined texts as compared to the descendant selector are recognised as the imediate children of the p.child-selector-eg element.

Adjascent Sibling Selector

Example:-

This are my descendants: Jackob, Lea, Nebuchadneza and Mr. Ndegwa. They are too many, i almost forgot the others. We have Mercy, Ruth and Kimberly.

Tomy

The above underlined text is recognised to be after the p.adjascent-sibling-selector-eg element and having the same class property as it's imediate children.

General Sibling Selector

Example:-

This are my descendants: Jackob, Lea, Nebuchadneza and Mr. Ndegwa. They are too many, i almost forgot the others. We have Mercy, Ruth and Kimberly.

Tony Sania

The above underlined texts are recognised to be after the p.general-sibling-selector-eg element and having the same class property as it's imediate children.
